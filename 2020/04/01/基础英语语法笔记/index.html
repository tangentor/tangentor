代词的主格和宾格

主格代词： I he she it you we they

例：I am a teacher

宾格代词： me him her it you us them

例：He likes me

名词性/形容词性物主代词

形容词性物主代词：

单数：my your his/her/its one’s

复数：our,your,their

名词性物主代词：

单数形式：mine,yours,his,hers,its one’s

复数：ours，yours,theirs

反身代词

单数：myself,youself,herself,himself,itself

复数：youselves、ourselves、themselves

例子：

Please help youself to some fish 随便吃些鱼吧

We enjoyed ourselves last night

实意动词的特征：

例： come read go watch play fly

1.He comes from Shengyang 一般现在时的第三人称单数，动词加s

2.She is reading story books 现在进行时表示现在正在发生的事情

3.They went to America yesterday 过去时

动词应该有时态和数量的变化

实意动词的否定、提问、回答

一般现在时/一般过去时的否定：在助动词do does did 后面加not

例子：

I don’t go to school by bus

She doesn’t watch TV everyday

一般现在时的否定提问：使用助动词do does did 放在句首，若原句的时态发生变化，则需要使用原型

例子：

He often plays golf

Does he often play golf?

Yes,he does/No,he doesn’t

Sam had breakfast yesterday.

Did sam have breakfast yesterday?

使用疑问词进行提问和回答

疑问词：when where who what how

例子：

原句：He bought three books yesterday

缺失He：Who bought three books yesterday

缺失three books：What did he by yesterday

缺失yesterday：When did he by three books

其他疑问词： how long，how far how often why

例子：They have been in China for three years

​ How long have they been in China？

​ It is about 4 kilometers from Beijing to Xi’an

​ How far is it from Beijing to Xi’an

​ They come to visit me once a week

​ How often do they come to visit me
